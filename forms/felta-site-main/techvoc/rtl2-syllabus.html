Course Description

This course builds upon the foundational knowledge acquired in Robotics Technology Level I, focusing on advanced robotics concepts, industrial applications, and hands-on project development. Students will engage in practical exercises, industry-standard simulations, and real-world robotics projects.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  • Design and implement complex robotic systems using industry-standard tools and methodologies
  • Apply advanced kinematics and control systems in robotics applications
  • Develop and troubleshoot robotic programs for industrial automation
  • Collaborate effectively in team-based robotics projects
  • Demonstrate professional competence in robotics safety and maintenance
  • Integrate robotics systems with other industrial automation technologies

Course Policies

  • Attendance

    Regular attendance is mandatory. More than three unexcused absences may result in course failure. Lab sessions cannot be made up.

  • Late Work

    Assignments submitted after the due date will receive a 10% deduction per day, up to three days. No submissions accepted after three days.

  • Academic Integrity

    All work must be original. Plagiarism or cheating will result in immediate course failure and disciplinary action.

  • Lab Safety

    Strict adherence to safety protocols is required. Failure to follow safety guidelines will result in immediate removal from the lab.
