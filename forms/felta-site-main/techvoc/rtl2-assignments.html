Course Assignments

Robot Arm Kinematics Project

Due: March 12, 2025 Points: 100 Type: Project
Due in 2 days

Design and implement a robot arm kinematics system that can perform pick-and-place operations. The project should demonstrate understanding of forward and inverse kinematics, as well as proper error handling and safety protocols.

Required deliverables:

  • Complete project report (PDF format)
  • Source code files (.py or .cpp)
  • Documentation of testing procedures
  • Video demonstration (max 5 minutes)

Core Competencies Midterm Exam

Due: March 25, 2025 Points: 150 Type: Exam
Due in 15 days

The midterm exam will cover all topics from Modules 1-3, including robot kinematics, programming fundamentals, and safety protocols.

Exam format:

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Format: Multiple Choice and Problem Solving
  • Location: Main Computer Lab
  • Required Materials: Calculator, Student ID

Lab Safety Certification

Due: March 1, 2025 Points: 50 Type: Quiz
48/50

Complete the lab safety certification quiz to demonstrate understanding of safety protocols and emergency procedures in the robotics laboratory.

Topics covered:

  • Emergency procedures
  • Equipment safety guidelines
  • Personal protective equipment
  • Workspace organization
